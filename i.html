My Adventure in Sikkim

I recently fulfilled my dream of visiting Sikkim! This fascinating place is truly a land of wonders- from snowy mountains to border pass and various waterfalls.

  • Tsongmo Lake or Changgu Lake, is a glacial lake in Gangtok district

  • The lake surface reflects different colours with change of seasons.

  • The lake remains frozen during the winter season.

    • I found the Sikkim people to be friendly and welcoming. It's natural scenery is out of the world. Exploring it's beauty makes me feel like I am on another planet.
